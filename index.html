About

Memaree is a social media app that allows users to create and share content in a safe and private environment. With the ability to create Groups for collaborative content creation and the option to share posts with a select group of people in their Circle or the with the world in their Vision, Memaree provides a unique and versatile platform for social connection. Users can fearlessly express themselves and share memories with those closest to them, all while maintaining control over their privacy.
